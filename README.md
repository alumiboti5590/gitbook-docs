# Welcome

Welcome to the FRC Team 5590 documentation site. We are the Alumiboti, a [_FIRST_ Robotics Competition](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/frc/) team that started in 2015 and is based out of St. Joseph's Collegiate Institute in Buffalo, New York.

This documentation is designed for our team members to better understand our processes, culture, and best practices so we can all elevate each other's abilities and build the best robots possible! It is made public in the case that any amount of documentation may help your team succeed in some way - or maybe you just find this kind of content interesting?
